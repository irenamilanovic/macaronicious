Who are we?

We have been with you since 2009. We are expanding, growing, inventing new treats, providing the joy of giving and enjoying every sweet moment with you. We are a famous patisserie shop and we can proudly claim, much loved French macarons, founded as a spontaneous and wanted extension of a small family manufacture. We started making desserts without much fuss. Today we own substantial production facilities and we can host you in our patisserie shop.

Who do we do?

We produce desserts out of supreme quality ingredients, inspired by French dessert-making tradition.. From our ovens and stoves directly to our showcases, we send macaroons in more than twenty flavors. To your celebrations, we deliver unforgettable macarons that you love. Open a box of macaroons and you will be swept by colors. Try just one and you won’t be able to forget them.
